Welcome to the official **CordSync** wiki!

CordSync is a premium, powerful, and highly modular Minecraft-Discord account linking system. This plugin offers seamless integration, allowing your players to interact perfectly between your server and Discord community.

## 🚀 Key Features

- **Robust 2FA Login System**: Secure your players' accounts! Players must approve logins via a Discord DM from your bot when they join the Minecraft server.
- **Webhook Chat Bridge**: Send Minecraft chat to Discord using Webhooks, setting the player's beautiful Minecraft skin as their Discord avatar automatically.
- **In-Game Premium GUI**: A stunning, fully configurable chest menu (`/link`) allowing players to link, check their status, view rewards, or unlink safely.
- **Console Bridge & Sync**: Control your Minecraft server effortlessly from within a dedicated, role-restricted Discord channel.
- **Reverse Role-Sync**: Instantly grant Minecraft LuckPerms ranks or groups when a user buys Nitro or receives a specific Discord role.
- **PlaceholderAPI (PAPI) Support**: Over 8 dynamic placeholders for scoreboards, tablists, and menus (e.g., `%cordsync_is_linked%`).
- **Multi-Server & Folia Ready**: Completely compatible with Spigot, PaperMC, Purpur, Leaf, and optimized for Folia schedulers out-of-the-box.
- **MySQL & SQLite**: Start instantly with blazing-fast local SQLite storage, or switch to remote MySQL flat-file for BungeeCord/Velocity setups.
- **Multi-Language (Locales)**: 100% localized messages. English, Turkish, German, Spanish, and French included by default (`en.yml`, `tr.yml`, etc.) with fallback protection support.
